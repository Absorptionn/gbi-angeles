The three stars represent the three main islands of the Philippines, namely: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

The sword symbolizes power, might, and honor.

The scale of justice represents just and uprightness.

The interlocking hands symbolize intimacy among members.

The laurel wreath represent the different places where members are residing.

GBI acronym of the title of the organization.

The golden yellow color represents the sun shining the noble aims and purposes of the organization.

The red and blue colors represent the Philippine flag signifying nationalism.

Inside the circle are the six (6) guiding principles of brotherhood, equality, justice, peace, service, and discipline, underlying the tenets and ideals of GUARDIANS Brotherhood Inc.
