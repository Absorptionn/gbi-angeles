THE  TEN GBI DOCTRINAL TEACHINGS

PARENTHOOD OF GOD

THAT ALL MEN CAME FROM ONE GOD. ALL HAVE COMMON NATURE AND COMMON DESTINY, OWING EERYTHNG FROM HIM WHO IS THE MOST MERCIFUL, MOST GRACIOUS AND MOST LOVING OF ALL MANKIND. AS THE LIFE AND LAW-GIVER, GOD IS THE MASTER FOUNDER OF ALL BROTHERHOOD ON EARTH.

BROTHERHOOD OF MEN

THAT ALL MEN ARE BROTHERS. NO RACIAL DISCRIMINATIONS. ALL BELONG TO THE FAMILY OF MEN, PRE-DESTINED TO LIVE TOGETHER IN HARMONY AS ONE BIG HAPPY FAMILY ON EARTH.

LOVE AND RESPECT FOR BROTHERS

LOVING IS THE CARDINAL RULE AND RESPECTING THE DIGNITY OF YOUR BROTHERS AS MAN, REGARDLESS OF SOCIAL STATUS IN LIFE IS A MORAL OBLIGATION AND FRATERNAL RESPONSIBILITY.

HUMILITY AND MEEKNESS

BE HUMBLE. FROM DUST YOU CAME AND TO DUST YOU WILL RETURN, KEEP THIS AS A LIVING AND VIBRANT MAXIM IN THE CULTURE OF OUR BROTHERHOOD. THIS IS OUR TRADEMARK, THE REAL POSSESSION IN THE CHANGING FORTUNES OF TIMES.

SERVANTHOOD

SERVICE TO GOD, SERVING OUR COUNTRY, BROTHERS AND HUMANITY AS A WHOLE IN THE SPIRIT OF VOLUNTEERISM WITHOUT EXPECTING ANY RETURN,

RESPECT OF DULY CONSTITUTED GOVERNMENT

THE AUTHORITY OF THE GOVERNMENT EMANATES FROM THE PEOPLE THE VOICE OF THE PEOPLE IS THE VOICE OF GOD.

ACTS OF CHARITY

IT IS ONLY BY GIVING THAT WE RECEIVE. GIVE WHAT IS DUE TO OUR BROTHERS, TO OUR ORGANIZATION, TO OUR COUNTRY AND TO OUR GOD.

ACTIVE AND PEACEFUL RESISTANCE AGAINST A CORRUPT AND TYRANNICAL GOVERNMENT

BEARING OUT THE INVIOLABLE AND UNALIENABLE RIGHTS OF AN INDIVIDUAL CLOTHED BY GOD AND EXERCISING THE RIGHT TO SELF-DEFENSE.

EQUALITY OF MEN

THAT ALL MEN ARE CREATED EQUAL THIS GOD GIVEN RIGHT MUST BE RESPECTED REGARDLESS OF HIS SOCIAL STATUS. MEN ARE NOT PROPERTIES OF THE STATE BUT UNDER THE THRESHOLD OF GOD, THE STRONG MUST NOT ENSLAVE THE WEAK, EQUAL PROTECTION OF THE LAW AND DUE PROCESS MUST BE FOLLOWED.

PEACE MAKING

PEACEMAKERS ARE THE SONS OF GOD. THAT A BROTHER MUST THINK, SPEAK AND ACT MOREOVER LEARN HOW TO PRACTICE THE REAL BROTHERHOOD, IN THIS RESPECT ATTAIN INNER TRANQUILITY AND FINALLY WORLD PEACE.
