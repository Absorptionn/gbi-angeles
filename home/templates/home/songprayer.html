Prayer

God, teach us to be courageous and not overcome by fear, discipline with the lowly and the high, Brotherly among friends and enemies, righteous in the face Creed. God, teach us to be ingenious protector of the Helpless, dauntless, advocator of the Filipino banner, liberty and oneness, kind, expecting neither thanks nor hatred. Loyal to our country and creed, mild in angers and fears. Noble in victory or defeat. Sincere without pretense, without pride. God, we beseech thee. Grant us wisdom to know the way, the light to show the way, the strength to go away. Amen.
