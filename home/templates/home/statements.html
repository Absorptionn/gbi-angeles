Mission

GBI - Angeles City is a fraternity of men and women who are committed to the tenets of self development, brotherhood, and charity for the betterment of society and its people of various color, persuasion, and creed.

Vision

To be the leading fraternal organization composed of well-disciplined members, promoting nationalism and patriotism. It shall strive that in due course it shall be a great partner for nation building and progress of the government.
